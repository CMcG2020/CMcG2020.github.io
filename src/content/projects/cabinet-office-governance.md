## Overview

Working closely with the Head of Data Management at the Cabinet Office, I contributed to numerous data governance and quality initiatives across UK government datasets.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Data Quality Assessments**: Led validation checks across government datasets, improving accuracy and consistency for policy and reporting use-cases
- **Governance Policies**: Authored and reviewed data governance and management policies, influencing data standards across teams
- **Tableau Partnership Management**: Acted as Tableau partnership manager, coordinating vendors, internal stakeholders, and delivery timelines
- **Reporting Initiatives**: Supported data analysis and reporting initiatives used by senior management

## Skills Developed

- Data quality frameworks and assessment methodologies
- Policy writing and documentation standards
- Vendor and stakeholder management
- Cross-team collaboration in government settings

## Impact

Contributed to improved data quality standards that enhanced the accuracy and reliability of government reporting and policy decisions.
